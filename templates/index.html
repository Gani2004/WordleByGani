Game Rules

  • Guess the 5-letter word in 6 tries.
  • A = Correct letter & spot
  • A = Correct letter, wrong spot
  • A = Not in the word
  • Use the on-screen or physical keyboard to play.

How to Play

Guess the 5-letter word in 6 tries.

  • Each guess must be a valid word.
  • After each guess, tiles show how close you were:
  • A Correct letter, correct spot
  • A Correct letter, wrong spot
  • A Letter not in the word
